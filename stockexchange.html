Stock Exchnage: is a market for financial instruments such as stocks. It is place where sellers sell their stocks and buyers buy the stocks.

There are total 16 stock exchanges in the world that have a market capitalization of over US $ 1 trillion each. There reffered to as the "$1 Trillion Club"

In 2015 these 16 exchnages accounted for 87% of global market capitalization, reports investopedia
